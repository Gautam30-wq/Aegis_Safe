Understanding the Challenge

The Struggles Women Face Every Day

Across the globe, women continue to face systemic barriers that limit their potential. From unequal pay and workplace discrimination to gender-based violence and restricted access to education and healthcare, millions of women struggle against deeply entrenched societal norms.

These challenges are not isolated, they are interconnected. Addressing one issue creates a ripple effect that uplifts communities and transforms entire nations. Awareness is the first step toward meaningful change.

Key Issues

Challenges That Define Our Fight

Understanding the key issues is the first step toward building a world where every woman can thrive.

Gender Inequality

Women earn 77 cents for every dollar men earn globally. Systemic barriers in workplaces, politics, and legal systems continue to limit opportunities.

Violence Against Women

1 in 3 women worldwide have experienced physical or sexual violence. Domestic abuse, trafficking, and harassment remain pervasive.

Health Disparities

Millions of women lack access to reproductive healthcare, maternal care, and mental health support, especially in underserved communities.

Education Gap

129 million girls worldwide are out of school. Cultural norms, poverty, and early marriage prevent girls from reaching their potential.
